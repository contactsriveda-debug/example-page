Important Instructions for Registration

  • Please fill the registration form carefully. Check all the details once before you submit.
  • Double-check your parent’s mobile number — make sure it is correct.
  • After you complete registration, you will receive your login details through a message (and on screen).
  • If you want to get your username and password immediately, please send us a message on WhatsApp through the Contact page.
  • Once you get the message, go to the Dashboard page and login using your details.
  • If you have any doubts or need help, you can contact us or send a message on WhatsApp anytime.
